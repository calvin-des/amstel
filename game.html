IT'S RAINING LEMON DROPS!

HOW TO PLAY

Move your Amstel Lager bottle to capture as many lemon drops as you can, transforming your Lager into a Radler before time runs out!

RULES:

  • Missing drops will deduct (X) seconds/milliseconds from your countdown.
  • Catching drops will add (X) seconds/milliseconds from your countdown.
  • Fill up the progress bar to be a potential prize winner
  • The drops will fall faster the longer you play.
